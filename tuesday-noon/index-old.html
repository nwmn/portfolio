About Me

I'm a second year undergraduate living in Northern California. My time is spent between San Francisco and Davis for school. Outside of class, I serve on an Ex-Officio Commission of ASUCD. In the past, I've also worked as a tutor for lower division computer science at the Student Academic Success Center in Dutton Hall. With my spare time, I enjoy volunteering at the LGBTQIA Resource Center and building websites for student organizations.
